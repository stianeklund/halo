# Work selection

When choosing work rather than receiving a fixed target:

Prefer:
1. frontier-first recovery
2. object-first recovery
3. leaf functions
4. small helpers
5. quick wins that reduce uncertainty around active .obj recovery

Do not prefer:
- broad speculative semantic hunts
- large refactors
- weakly bounded exploratory work when smaller confirmations are available

Advisory tooling may inform selection, but the binary outranks everything.
